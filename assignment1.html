2. Explain in a few sentences, why these results are different. Which is the least correct?

The differences are due to the way the projection of Earth’s surface is transformed and, also, the difference in the calculation method. There’s little difference among a, c, and d and a large difference in b (Pseudo-Mercator). This last is also the least correct, since the way the projection occurs is not appropriate for making spatial operations.
